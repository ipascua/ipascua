Introduction

The Pilipino Cultural Nights

The Pilipino Cultural Nights is a digital magazine that features the annual show productions entitled Pilipino Cultural Night. Pilipino Cultural Night is an annual show production organized by Filipino-American (Fil-Am) organizations in high schools and college universities that celebrates Filipino culture through various artistic performances such as music, dance, theater, and storytelling. This magazine aims to highlight the Pilipino Cultural Nights organized by Pilipino Cultural Exchange at Cal Poly SLO.
